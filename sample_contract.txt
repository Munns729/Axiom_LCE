FOUNDER SHARE AGREEMENT

Section 1. Definitions
1.4 "Good Reason" shall mean (i) a material reduction in salary, (ii) relocation of the Company's headquarters by more than 50 miles, or (iii) a material reduction in duties.

Section 4. Termination
4.2 Voluntary Resignation. If Founder voluntarily resigns for any reason, Founder shall be deemed a Bad Leaver and shall forfeit all shares, whether vested or unvested.

Section 5. Change of Control
5.1 Single Trigger Acceleration. In the event of a Change of Control, 100% of unvested shares shall immediately vest.
